Why I program

The drastic (and inevitable) change in our economy leads me to understand the need for computer programming skills. Since computers can perform repetitive jobs more efficient than any human it is necessary for human labor to switch from menial monotonous labor to more creative and problem solving oriented labor. I believe computer programming is a useful skill that allows us to use our creativity to better solve real world problems. That is my aim in life. To be a problem solver.
